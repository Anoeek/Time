My Time

Time is a concept so all encompassing, so larger than life that even the brightest among us can only relate and describe. There are many signs time is something inherent to us, like an internal metronome which guides us. However, the social construct of time we have set up to guide us in our daily lives, can sometimes clash with our internal metronome.
We aim to deconstruct the conventional measurements of time and reconsider our sense of time. Because time is such an intimate experience, magnifying your highs and lows, we’ve decided to reconsider it in a personal frame. In place of the conventional units like seconds, minutes and hours, we provide you with our own time units. They are based on private experiences, habits, hobbies, etc. You can take these as a base to construct your own time unit and add to a growing collection of more connected measurements.
